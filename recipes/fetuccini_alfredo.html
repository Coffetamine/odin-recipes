Fettucini Alfredo

Home

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is a pasta dish consisting of fettuccine tossed with butter and Parmesan cheese, which melt and emulsify to form a rich cheese sauce coating the pasta.

Ingredients
  • 8 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 4 ½ tablespoons butter
  • 2 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 5 drops hot pepper sauce
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
